<h2 style="font-size: 2.5rem; font-weight: 800; margin-bottom: 1.5rem; border-bottom: 4px solid var(--highlight-color); display: inline-block;">Manifesto</h2>
<p>The internet is dying under the boot of surveillance capitalism. Five corporations own your digital existence. They've turned "the cloud" into a euphemism for "rent-seeking middleman" and "user-friendly" into code for "we control everything."</p>
<ul style="list-style: none; padding: 0; margin-top: 2rem;">
<li style="margin-bottom: 1rem;"><strong>Self-hosting everything</strong> – Your email, your repos, your files, your life. If you're paying monthly rent for storage that sits on someone else's hardware, you're a serf in digital feudalism.</li>
<li style="margin-bottom: 1rem;"><strong>Building with open source exclusively</strong> – Proprietary software is a cage. The source code is the key. Anyone telling you otherwise profits from your captivity.</li>
<li style="margin-bottom: 1rem;"><strong>Burning down surveillance capitalism</strong> – Every "free" service is a panopticon. Stop volunteering for your own surveillance.</li>
<li style="margin-bottom: 1rem;"><strong>Weaponizing self-reliance</strong> – Learn to run your own infrastructure or accept that you'll always be at the mercy of some corporation's quarterly earnings call.</li>
</ul>
<p style="font-weight: 700; margin-top: 2rem; font-size: 1.5rem; text-align: center; font-style: italic; color: var(--tw-prose-headings);">"The cloud isn't the future. It's a scam. Host your own goddamn servers."</p>
</section>
